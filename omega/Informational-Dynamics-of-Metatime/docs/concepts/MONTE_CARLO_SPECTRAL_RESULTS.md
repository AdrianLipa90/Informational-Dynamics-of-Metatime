# MONTE_CARLO_SPECTRAL_RESULTS

The V5 layer shows that the orbital repository model has a real phase region rather than a single lucky point.

Key findings:
- there exists a Monte Carlo region improving both global closure metrics and spectral organization,
- sigma is the strongest driver of objective degradation/improvement, mostly through closure_penalty,
- dt is the dominant driver of R_H inflation,
- gamma and beta act as secondary spectral stabilizers,
- spectral observables (spectral radius, spectral gap, Fiedler value) provide a nonlocal view of stability not visible from single scalar metrics alone.
