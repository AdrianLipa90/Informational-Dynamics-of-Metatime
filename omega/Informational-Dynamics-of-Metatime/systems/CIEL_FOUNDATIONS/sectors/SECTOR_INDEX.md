# Sector Index

This index records how sector realizations must inherit from foundations.

## Inheritance rule
No sector may introduce independent constants or local axioms that bypass the foundations layer.
A sector must point back to:
- axioms/postulates,
- definitions,
- constants,
- operators,
- tests/falsification,
- artifacts/results.
