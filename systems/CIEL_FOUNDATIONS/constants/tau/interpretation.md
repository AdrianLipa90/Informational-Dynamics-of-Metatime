# Interpretation of tau

Operational role:
- transport / spectral mode of a sector,
- resonance coordinate inside $A_{ij}$,
- contraction coordinate in the closure relation,
- bridge between arithmetic seed structure and effective phase transport.

Limits of interpretation:
- the repo still contains at least two tau branches with different numbers,
- `tau_i` is close in role to older `lambda_i`, but not yet canonically identical,
- the current derivation supports `lambda_i = f(tau_i)` more strongly than `lambda_i = tau_i`.
